Trash Can: One "groovin" band

In the township of Osgoode, which is located in south eastern Ontario (yes that is in Canada) lives a group of four musicians who play together in a band which goes under the infamous name of Trash Can. We consist of 4 members who are very talented. We just released our first ever CD on Marvin Hearted Records, aptly named "Name that Stain." The band continues to thrive since its' debut into the music scene over 3.29 years ago. We hope that you will enjoy this informative and interesting web page about our band and that you will consider listening to some of our music (whether it be sound clips from this page, or a purchase of a tape or CD from us). If by any chance you do wish to contact us for any reason please feel free to e-mail any of the members of the band to get in touch with us. Thank you for taking the time to read this and please e-mail us with any questions or comments you may have.
